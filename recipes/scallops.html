Scallops with Pesto Cream Sauce Recipe

Scallops are a delicious shellfish, similar to clams or mussels. For this recipe, we will be searing them and serving them with a pesto cream sauce. Scallops pair nicely with bread, rice, or pasta.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Scallops
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon pesto
  • 1 tablespoon capers
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:

  1. Season the scallops with salt and pepper, then dredge them in flour.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet on medium heat, then add the scallops.
  3. Cook for a minute and a half on both sides.
  4. Add the pesto and capers and mix.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and bring it to a boil.
  6. Bon Appetite!
