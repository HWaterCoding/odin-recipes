Lasanga Recipe

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets. The same-named Italian dish is made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices

Ingredients:

  • Noodles (sheet)
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Cheese (Parmesan, Mozzarella, Ricotta)
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spice and Seasonings of your choice( Recommended: Basil, salt, pepper, parsley, italian seasoning, fennel seeds.)

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Proper Layer Format:

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat Layers, garnish with spices and parmesan cheese on top.
